Cristiano Ronaldo

Portuguese professional footballer

About Him:


Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on 5 February 1985 in Madeira, Portugal. He plays as a forward for Italian club Juventus and the Portugal national team. Commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo or CR7, he is regarded as one of the best players of this generation. He has won the FIFA Ballon d’Or five times, equalled only by rival Lionel Messi. Known for his pace and physicality, Ronaldo plays on the left wing, but can play as a striker as well. The Portuguese international has scored 555 goals from 749 club appearances till date. .
